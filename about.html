Hi! My name is Kevin and welcome to my blog. I decided to dedicate this blog to journalling my journey to play in the fabled Halford Hewitt competition, where 640 former private school golfers descend upon England's most beautiful stretch of golf courses.

In addition to golf I also love a variety of other sports including football (Chelsea), NFL (Broncos) and F1.

I'm also a big travel person. I've been to a lot of countries including playing golf in the USA, South Africa, Cyprus, Jamaica, Barbados, St Kitts and Nevis, Spain, Portugal and Morocco.

  • Best ever round: 1 over at Secret Valley golf course in Cyprus, 2016
  • Favourite Golf Course: Royal St Georges
  • Favourite club in the bag: Driver
  • Strengths: Driving, Ball striking, creativity
  • Weaknesses: Lag putting, approach shots inside 120
